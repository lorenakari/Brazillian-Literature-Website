Brazilian Literature

Literature is one of the parts of a culture that reflects its multi-dimensional nature. Writers use their medium to communicate with others, record history, convey ideas and depict an identity. Therefore, literature is an integral part of a country’s complex culture.
Brazilian literature is, as a result, extremely multifaceted, making it a fascinating record of people, places and times. Just like its people, Brazilian literature comes from a place of great struggle and pain to create the most beautiful and complex things.
